StockholmJS meetup talk - Workflow Driven Development
Wed, May 4, 2016
1 minute read

Couple days ago I gave my first ever talk at StockholmJS meetup. Topic was “Workflow Driven Development”. It is about architectural choices we’ve made for our new frontend at Nordnet and why we did them. It was great to see how ideas we have for our frontend architecture are also interesting to other developers. I’ve got some good feedback and interesting questions afterwards.

Slides for the talk could be found here - Workflow Driven Development. Maybe there’s even going to be a video of my talk later.
